LOPEZ POBLACION was divided into five districts after it founded in 1856, Burgos, Rizal, Gomez, Talolong and Magsaysay. Presently the five districts its name from former name Lopez which is Talolong. It was also named after its most historical place in this town; the Talolong River, wherein the forefather and early settlers of Lopez used this river as the gateway for trade and commerce of this town before 1856. Early settlers traveled to the neighboring towns such as Gumaca, Atimonan, Alabat and Mauban using the “paraws” and small sailboats. Talolong is located on the Eastern part of Lopez with the land area of 247,057.27. It is now composed of six (6) zones. It is bounded on the North by Barangay Rizal, on the East Barangay Magsaysay and the Talolong River, on the South by Barangay Vilahermosa and Boc-Boc on the Southwest and on the West by Barangay Burgos. There are several Barangay Captains who served and improved this place, particularly: Segundo Datario, Luisito Navarro, Felixberto Abines, Remegio O. Rico, Felixberto O. Villegas, Aurea Z. Villafuerte-OIC, Nolasco E. Capanzana, Rosalinda V. Rico, Dohmie R. Marquez and Aristedes R. Yumul, Jr.- 2013 up to present.

Mission and Vision

Misyon ng Barangay

Maiangat ang antas ng pamumuhay ng mga mamamayan at mapanatili
ang isang mapayapang pamayanan.

Pananaw ng Barangay

Isang pamayanang nagkakaisa, sumusunod sa mga alituntuning pinaiiral ng mga nakatataas at sama-sama sa pagtuklas ng mga positibo at alternatibong paraan para sa ika-uunlad ng barangay. Mas maganda, mapayapa at nagkakaisang pamayanan tungo sa ika-uunlad at may dekalidad na pamumuhay.
